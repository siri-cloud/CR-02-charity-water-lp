Our Mission

At charity: water, our mission is simple: to bring clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. We believe in a world where everyone has access to life’s most basic need. We’re committed to transparency, innovation, and empowering local communities with sustainable solutions.

Why Water?

Water is essential to life, yet over 703 million people around the world lack access to clean water. Without it, communities face countless challenges — from poor health and hunger to limited education and economic opportunities. Clean water not only saves lives, it creates opportunities for a better future.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
